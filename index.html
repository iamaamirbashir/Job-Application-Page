Software Engineering Intern

We are looking for a motivated Software Engineering Intern to join our frontend team and contribute to the development of user interfaces and client-side applications. This is a remote/hybrid opportunity that offers hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies.

Duration: Unpaid for the first three months, potential for a stipend for the following six months based on performance, with the possibility of full-time employment.

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with the frontend development team to design, develop, and maintain user interfaces using React.js.
  • Implement responsive and accessible designs that provide an exceptional user experience across various devices and browsers.
  • Utilize data fetching and management & state management libraries such as React-Query and Zustand for efficient data fetching and state management.
  • Integrate UI components from Material-UI to maintain consistency and improve development efficiency.
  • Write clean, modular, and reusable code following best practices and coding standards.
  • Participate in code reviews, provide feedback, and address any issues or bugs.
  • Stay updated on frontend development trends, tools, and techniques.

Requirements:

  • Strong understanding of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.
  • Proficiency in React.js and its ecosystem, including data fetching-cum-management and state management libraries like React-Query and Zustand.
  • Familiarity with Material-UI for building responsive and visually appealing user interfaces.
  • Solid understanding of frontend development concepts, such as component-based architecture, responsive design, and web accessibility.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Effective communication skills and the ability to work collaboratively in a remote or hybrid environment.
  • Self-motivated with a passion for learning and professional growth.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with frontend build tools like Webpack, Babel, or Parcel.
  • Familiarity with testing frameworks such as Jest and React Testing Library.
  • Knowledge of version control systems, particularly Git.
  • Previous internship or project experience in frontend development.
  • Understanding of agile development methodologies.

Benefits:

  • Gain hands-on experience in frontend development with a focus on React.js.
  • Exposure to modern frontend technologies and tools.
  • Mentorship and guidance from experienced professionals in the field.
  • Flexible remote/hybrid work arrangements.
  • Potential for a stipend after the initial three-month period based on performance.
  • Possibility of full-time employment based on performance and business needs.
